About EV

Data analytics has become increasingly important in the electric vehicle (EV) industry in the modern era. It helps EV manufacturers to analyze driving patterns, battery performance, and charging behavior to optimize vehicle design and improve range. Environmental benefits:

  • EVs produce zero emissions
  • Lower operating costs
  • Quieter operation

Data analytics can help identify areas for improvement in charging infrastructure, such as the locations of charging stations and the types of chargers available.
